# How we treat each other here.

Lumina is built on one belief:

**Everyone's story deserves to be shared without fear.**

That means everyone's. The quiet voice and the loud one. The joyful story and the difficult one. The perspective you agree with and the one you don't.

To keep this a space where that's possible, we have a few principles. They're not complicated.

---

## Be honest. Be kind. Be human.

**Honest** means sharing what's real for you, not what sounds impressive or what you think people want to hear.

**Kind** doesn't mean only saying positive things. You can share pain, frustration, anger. Kindness means not directing that at another person to hurt them.

**Human** means remembering that behind every story is a real person who took a risk by sharing it.

---

## What we don't allow.

We remove content that:

- Targets, threatens, or degrades another person
- Contains hate speech based on race, gender, religion, sexuality, disability, or any other identity
- Is designed to harass, bully, or intimidate
- Is spam or commercial promotion
- Includes private information about someone without their consent
- Glorifies self-harm or encourages others toward it

We approach removals with care, not trigger-happiness. Context matters. We read before we act.

---

## What we protect.

We protect:

- Honest accounts of hard experiences, including grief, trauma, mental health struggles, and relationship pain
- Disagreement and critical thinking, even when uncomfortable
- Anonymity — you can share without your name
- Your right to delete your story at any time

---

## How to report something.

If you see something that feels wrong, use the "Report this" option on any story. We read every report.

You won't be penalised for reporting. We take it seriously.

---

## A note on disagreement.

We are a community of different lives, different beliefs, and different experiences. You will read things you don't agree with. That's a feature, not a bug.

The response to disagreement here is to share your own perspective — not to silence someone else's.
